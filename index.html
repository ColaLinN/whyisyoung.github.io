About me

I'm a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at Virginia Tech since 08/2018. My advisor is Dr. Gang Wang. I'm particularly interested in the application of Data Mining, Machine Learning, and Security.

I received a B.Eng in Computer Science from East China Normal University (ECNU) in 2015. From May 2013 to June 2015, I was a member of ECNU Innovative Talents Class Committee. During my master's study at ECNU, I am a recipient of ECNU Graduate Student Overseas Research Scholarship. Before joining Virginia Tech, I also spent a couple of enjoyable months under the supervision of Dr. Xinyu Xing and Dr. Gang Wang at Penn State in 2017.

Internship

  • Search and Rank Intern @Wenba, 09/2016 – 10/2016
    • Located possible reasons of response bottleneck, read the whole source code related to searching of XuebaJun app (one of the top three apps in its area), and accomplished a comprehensive code report.
  • Exploit Intern @ Peking University, 07/2015 - 08/2015
    • Supervised by Ph.D Candidate Yu Ding and learned binary vulnerability discovery/exploit (Windows), Android security (APK unpacking and repackaging), Web security (XSS).
  • Quantitative Analyst Assistant Intern @UnionPay Smart, 03/2015 – 06/2015
    • Using Hadoop clusters to fetch luxury industry data from the transaction records of 2.7 billion credit cards (supported by China UnionPay), written with Python and Shell scripts.
